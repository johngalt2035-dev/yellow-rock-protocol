Who Is This For?

Anyone dealing with high-conflict individuals, groups, or business entities who communicates via Signal, Telegram, WhatsApp, or SMS. The system drafts appropriate responses using proven psychology — you review and send.

How It Works

1. They message youIncoming message on Signal, Telegram, WhatsApp, or SMS
2. EA drafts responseYour AI Executive Assistant generates a BIFF-compliant draft using the Grey Rock protocol
3. Invisible side-channel reviewDraft routed to your private review channel (separate Signal/Telegram/WhatsApp/SMS thread). You approve, edit, reject, or escalate. Multi-reviewer chains supported for organizations.
4. You approve & sendOnly approved responses are sent to the contact via the original channel. Contact has zero visibility into the review process.
5. Everything archivedBoth sides + all review actions stored with forensic SHA-256 hashes. Configurable retention (1-7+ years).

Invisible Side-Channel Review

Every draft goes through a private review channel before reaching the contact. The contact never sees drafts, edits, reviewer comments, or any evidence of the review process.

Single Reviewer

Principal receives draft via private Signal/Telegram/WhatsApp/SMS thread. Reply to approve, edit, reject, or regenerate.

Multi-Reviewer Chain

Principal → Legal Counsel → Final Approver. Sequential review via each reviewer's private channel. All required reviewers must approve.

Message Integrity & Forensic Signing

Every approved message is cryptographically signed with SHA-256 at every stage of the pipeline. Approved text is immutable — zero modifications between approval and delivery. Maximum truthfulness enforced.

Forensic Signing Chain

StageHashWhat It Proves
1. DraftSHA-256(draft_id | contact_id | content | timestamp)Draft content is recorded at creation
2. ApprovalSHA-256(draft_hash | reviewer_id | action | timestamp)Who approved, when, with what content
3. SendSHA-256(sent_content | send_timestamp | channel)Exact content delivered to contact
4. Verifydraft_content == sent_contentCharacter-for-character match. Mismatch → BLOCKED
5. ArchiveSHA-256(sent_hash | archive_timestamp)Permanent forensic record in Grey Rock Memory

Immutable Drafts

Approved text cannot be modified. No greetings, sign-offs, emojis, or "softening" added after approval. The approved message IS the sent message.

Maximum Truthfulness

Every factual claim traces to: (1) the incoming message, (2) a verified memory record, or (3) principal-approved training data. No other sources. Ever.

Hallucination Gates

Uncertain claims flagged [UNVERIFIED] + [PRINCIPAL_REVIEW]. Never included in drafts without explicit principal approval.

Privileged Comms

Absolute separation between review channel and contact channel. Attorney-client privilege, work product, reviewer notes — zero leakage. Cannot be gamed.

The Protocol

BIFF Standard

Brief. Informative. Friendly. Firm. Every response passes the "judge test" — safe for court.

Anti-JADE

Never Justify, Argue, Defend, or Explain. Shuts down argument loops before they start.

Anti-DARVO

Detects Deny-Attack-Reverse Victim & Offender manipulation. Multi-level response ladder.

Provocation Filter

Ignores emotional bait. Extracts logistics. Responds only to facts.

Legal Framework

"Judge test" on every message. No fault admissions, no threats, no sarcasm. Court-safe.

Pattern Recognition

12 manipulation patterns with calibrated responses. Gaslighting, triangulation, love-bombing, and more.

Evidence-Based

Grounded in behavioral psychology and HCI research. Full citations with honest limitations. See Research Basis.

Chain-of-Thought Reasoning

8-step structured reasoning chain. Classify → Pattern → Memory → Protocol → Style → Judge Test → Gate → Draft.

Simulation Scenarios

Zero Deviation Policy

Privileged Communications
